About This Resort

Monal Resort is perched high in the Margalla Hills, offering breathtaking panoramic views of Islamabad city. This hilltop retreat combines traditional Pakistani architecture with modern luxury, featuring elegant cottages and rooms designed for comfort. The resort is famous for its spectacular sunset views, fine dining restaurant, and serene mountain atmosphere. It's the perfect escape from city life while remaining close to the capital's conveniences.
