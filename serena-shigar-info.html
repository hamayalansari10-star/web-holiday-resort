About This Resort

Serena Shigar Fort is a magnificent 400-year-old restored palace that perfectly blends historical architecture with modern luxury. Located in the beautiful Shigar Valley, this heritage property offers a unique experience of living in royal splendor while being surrounded by the mighty Karakoram mountains. The fort features traditional Balti and Tibetan architectural elements, ornate woodwork, and beautifully landscaped gardens with apricot orchards.
