About This Resort

Nathia Gali Forest Resort is a charming mountain retreat nestled deep within ancient pine forests at an altitude of 8,000 feet. This eco-friendly resort features cozy wooden cottages that blend seamlessly with the natural surroundings, offering a peaceful escape from urban life. The resort is famous for its hiking trails, birdwatching opportunities, and fresh mountain air. Wake up to the sound of birds chirping and enjoy the magical experience of living among towering pine trees and misty mountains.
