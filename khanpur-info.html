About This Resort

Lakeside Resort Khanpur is a peaceful waterfront retreat located on the scenic shores of Khanpur Dam. Surrounded by lush greenery and crystal-clear turquoise waters, this resort offers a perfect blend of adventure and relaxation. The modern lakeside cottages provide stunning water views, making it an ideal destination for families, couples, and adventure enthusiasts. Enjoy water sports, boat rides, and serene sunsets in this tranquil paradise.
