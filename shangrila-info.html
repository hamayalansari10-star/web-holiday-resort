About This Resort

Shangrila Resort, also known as "Heaven on Earth," is nestled beside the stunning Lower Kachura Lake in Skardu. This iconic resort offers breathtaking views of crystal-clear waters surrounded by majestic mountains. The resort features traditional Balti architecture blended with modern comfort, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.
